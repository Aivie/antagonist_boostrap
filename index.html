GOTHEL

Gothel is the main antagonist from Walt Disney Picture's movie Tangled.

About

Gothel is an old witch who relies on the power of the healing power within Rapunzel's hair to stay young and alive. She pretends to be Rapunzel's mother and keeps her locked in a tower so that she can hoard Rapunzel's hair for herself.

Goal

Gothel's goal is to keep Rapunzel locked up so that Gothel can shelter Rapunzel from the rest of the world, so that only she is able to use her magical hair to keep herself young and alive.

Defeat

As all antagonists, Gothel is defeated eventually. In Tangled, she is defeated when Flynn Rider cuts off Rapunzel's hair, making it lose its magical properties and reverting Gothel back to her actual form.
